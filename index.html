About Us

Medicaputare is a health and pharmacology innovation company, designed from the ground-up to be at the forefront of digital technology development that enhances medication safety, personalizes therapy, and advances research in pharmacogenomics, therapeutic drug monitoring (TDM), and AI-driven pharmacology.

At Medicaputare, we believe technology should make healthcare more human. We design clinically intelligent tools that blend AI, pharmacogenomics, and digital innovation to guide safer, more personalized treatment. Our mission is to transform care by reducing adverse drug reactions and optimizing patient outcomes.

Our Mission & Vision

Medicaputare is dedicated to optimizing treatment outcomes and reducing adverse drug reactions through cutting-edge, clinically intelligent technologies. We envision a future where personalized medicine is accessible and effective for all.

With a focus on practical innovation, Medicaputare combines contextual insights and global expertise to deliver health solutions relevant to diverse populations. We are committed to the advancement of healthcare in Africa and beyond.

Innovation Pillars

Precision Medicine

Pharmacogenomics research & CDS integration.

  • Multi-gene PGx panel integration with CDSS
  • Personalized therapy for improved efficacy
  • Genotype-based risk stratification

AI in Pharmacology

Advancing drug discovery and precision dosing through AI-enabled pharmacology.

  • AI-driven PK/PD & toxicity modeling
  • Machine learning for drug discovery and development
  • Precise therapeutic drug monitoring and dose optimisation

Digital Health Tools

Exploring and prototyping digital tools to enhance healthcare delivery and patient management.

  • Medical devices & API services
  • Digital calculators & PGx applications
  • Adherence and clinical progress monitoring
